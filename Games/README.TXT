PROJECT TITLE: Flamingo of War
PURPOSE OF PROJECT: Greenfoot Project
AUTHORS: Khang Hoang - 60130500239

This is how to play the game!

The story: In the imagined planet of Greenfoot, A City and B City are facing a very dangerous threat called "The Giant Flamingo". They have no weapon to defeat it but they are able to push it to the another city. The game started when The Giant Flamingo appears in the border line of 2 cities, 2 players will try their best to win the other by pushing The Giant Flamingo to the other city.

Guide:
Player A: click "1" to push the Giant Flamingo to B City
Player B: click "left arrow" to push the Giant Flamingo to A City.
The winner is the player that pushing the Giant Flamingo to other city.

When Player A wins, we will hear "Hit me on the ground! Hit me on the ground! Hit me on the ground! Hit me on the ground!" sound because A City's citizens love boxing.
When Player B wins, we will hear "Database, Database, I'm struggling in the database, wow oh" because B City's citizens love coding.
